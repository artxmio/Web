Мои увлечения

As a designer and digital artist, my hobbies are deeply connected with creativity and visual storytelling. I love drawing — both digitally and on paper — and constantly explore different styles, from minimalism to complex illustrations. Art is more than just a pastime for me; it's a way to express emotions and bring ideas to life.

I also enjoy graphic design, especially creating posters, logos, and UI concepts. Working with color, composition, and typography brings me a lot of joy. I often experiment with design tools like Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, and Figma.

When I’m not drawing or designing, I get inspired by games, films, and music. I pay close attention to how visuals and sound work together to create mood and atmosphere — it helps me grow as both an artist and a designer.
